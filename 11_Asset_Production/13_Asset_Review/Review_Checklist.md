# Asset Review Checklist

Run every test. Note pass/fail with a one-sentence reason.

---

## 1. Cinematic Quality

- [ ] Could this still hold as a frame in a film?
- [ ] Is composition deliberate (rule of thirds / golden ratio)?
- [ ] Is there atmosphere (haze, depth, light falloff)?
- [ ] Does the frame breathe? At least 60% negative space (cinematic) or
      40% (editorial)?
- [ ] Does the lens choice make sense (no fisheye, no over-wide)?

**Fail criteria**: looks like stock photography, marketing collateral,
or a 2010s SaaS hero image.

---

## 2. Brand Alignment

- [ ] Two-temperature lighting (cool key, warm rim) present?
- [ ] Materials match SheetFlow vocabulary (matte ceramic, brushed graphite,
      pale concrete, frosted glass)?
- [ ] Color palette restrained — temperature contrast, not hue contrast?
- [ ] Typography (if present) follows
      [Typography moodboard](../04_Moodboards/Typography.md)?
- [ ] No banned elements: neon, RGB, holographic grids, lens flare,
      cyberpunk markers?

**Fail criteria**: any banned element. Any palette outside the SheetFlow
vocabulary without explicit brief override.

---

## 3. Atmosphere Restraint

- [ ] Is haze present at 4–6%? (Or thoughtfully absent.)
- [ ] Are shadows preserved as **dark**, not filled to gray?
- [ ] Is there one dominant light source (not a balanced three-point)?
- [ ] Is bloom absent or surgically deployed (not blanket)?
- [ ] Is volumetric light real (geometric beam) or fake (post bloom)?

**Fail criteria**: milky shadows, "cinematic LUT" bleach, fake bloom,
generic "atmosphere" presets.

---

## 4. Emotional Impact

- [ ] In two seconds, does it feel intelligent, expensive, calm?
- [ ] Does it provoke curiosity rather than excitement?
- [ ] Would a senior CEO show this without explaining?
- [ ] Does it whisper? Or does it shout?

**Fail criteria**: feels excited, urgent, salesy, or generically "tech."

---

## 5. Futuristic Elegance

- [ ] Could this image fit in a film made today AND a film made in 2035?
- [ ] Is the "futurism" implied through restraint, not through visual tropes?
- [ ] Are the geometry choices honest (forms with reason, not decoration)?
- [ ] Is there at most ONE "impossible" element per frame?

**Fail criteria**: visual tropes (HUD, grids, neon, holograms) standing
in for actual futurism.

---

## 6. The "Feels Like SheetFlow" Test

The hardest, most subjective, most important test.

- [ ] If you saw this without a logo, would you know it was a luxury intelligence brand?
- [ ] Does it sit comfortably next to [hero scenes](../01_Spline_Scenes/Hero_Intelligence_Core.md)?
- [ ] Apple test: would this fit on apple.com?
- [ ] Linear test: would this look loud on linear.app?
- [ ] Vercel test: would this look generic on vercel.com?

If yes/no/no/no — pass. Anything else — fail.

---

## Final outcome

- All 6 pass → **lock and ship**
- 1 fail with clear fix path → **iterate**
- 2+ fails OR brand-alignment fail → **reject and rebrief**

---

## Common rejection reasons (memorize these)

| Reason                                       | What it means                              |
|----------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| "Too much going on"                          | Multiple subjects competing                |
| "Filler haze"                                | Atmospheric look without atmospheric depth |
| "RGB rim light"                              | Color contamination from gel               |
| "Holiday lights"                             | Pinpoint highlights everywhere             |
| "Stock energy"                               | Composition is too safe / centered         |
| "Aging triangle"                             | Geometry trope dating from 2018            |
| "Selling, not living"                        | Composition performs for camera            |
| "Banner not film"                            | Reads as web banner, not editorial         |
