# Moodboard — Typography

## Direction
Type with mass. Words that carry the weight of architecture.

## References to study
- Apple keynote title cards
- Aesop packaging type
- Massimo Vignelli's NYC subway signage
- Wim Crouwel grid systems
- Linear's marketing typography
- Type specimens from Lineto, Klim, Grilli, Commercial Type
- Vanity Fair feature openings
- Movie title cards: Dunkirk, Arrival, There Will Be Blood

## Adjectives
massed · tracked · structural · editorial · breathing · intentional

## Anti-references
- Outline text on busy backgrounds
- Drop shadows
- Multiple display weights in one composition
- Italic for emphasis (use weight or scale instead)
- All-caps mixed with lowercase as decoration
- "AI typewriter" effects on hero copy

## Type rules (binding)
1. **One display weight per composition.**
2. **Tracking**: display -0.02 to -0.03em; caps +0.04em; body 0.
3. **Color**: white at 92% opacity, never pure white.
4. **Drop shadows are forbidden.** Fix the background instead.
5. **Hero scale**: clamp(56px, 8vw, 96px). Never bigger. Restraint.
6. **Line height**: display 1.05; body 1.55.
