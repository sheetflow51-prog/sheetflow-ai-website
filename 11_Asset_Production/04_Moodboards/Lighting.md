# Moodboard — Lighting

## Direction
One dominant source. One supporting rim. Everything else lives in honest shadow.

## References to study
- Roger Deakins — any frame from 1917, Blade Runner 2049, Skyfall
- Bradford Young — Arrival opening sequence
- Greig Fraser — Dune (2021) interior daylight scenes
- Hoyte van Hoytema — Her, the warm interior portraits
- Apple keynote product reveals — the locked top-key + black flag
- A Vermeer painting — north-window light, single source
- Caravaggio — for shadow side preservation
- Vanity Fair editorial portraits — Annie Leibovitz lighting

## Adjectives
sculpted · directional · honest · two-temperature · falling-off · expensive · quiet

## Anti-references
- Three-point flat newscaster lighting
- Ring light (kills depth on humans)
- RGB stage washes
- Bloom-substituting-for-volumetric
- "Cinematic" lens flare overlays

## SheetFlow lighting recipe
```
Key:     cool 5600–6000K, large soft source, top-front
Rim:     warm 3000–3200K, hard source, 90–120° behind
Fill:    minimal — bounce only, never an active source
Ambient: cool 6500K, very low intensity
Haze:    4–6% volumetric, neutral
```
