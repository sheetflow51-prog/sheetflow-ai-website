# Moodboard — Atmosphere

## Direction
The air has weight. The room is hushed. Something important is about to happen,
but nothing is rushing it.

## References to study
- Blade Runner 2049 — the foggy ovoid forest scene
- Arrival — the inside of the heptapod ship
- Foundation — the vault interior
- Apple Vision Pro reveal — the empty studio scenes
- 2001: A Space Odyssey — the white interior corridor
- Severance — the office at night, fluorescent + dark
- A still photograph of an empty cathedral at dawn
- The negative space between objects in Japanese tea ceremonies

## Adjectives
weighted · hushed · suspended · intentional · lit · still · expensive · alive

## Anti-references
- Smoke machine "atmosphere" with hard color light
- Cyberpunk wet street neon
- Sci-fi corridor with strobing alarms
- "Atmospheric" gaming intro cinematics

- Background plates for hero sections
