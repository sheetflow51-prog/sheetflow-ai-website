# Moodboard — Geometry

## Direction
Forms with reason. Nothing decorative. Curves where comfort matters,
edges where confidence does.

## References to study
- Apple Vision Pro industrial design
- Teenage Engineering OP-1 — geometry as identity
- Dieter Rams Braun catalog
- Naoto Fukasawa product work
- Linear's iconography
- Concrete Brutalist architecture (Kahn, Tadao Ando)
- Foundation set design (Apple TV+) — the council chamber
- Single icosahedron lit from above

## Adjectives
considered · soft-cornered · subdivided · weighted · proportioned · honest

## Anti-references
- Sharp triangles as decoration (overused since 2018)
- Hexagonal grids ("blockchain" or "tech")
- Wireframe globes
- Polygon "low-poly mountains"
- Arrow shapes pointing at things
- Gradient bubbles and blobs ("Gen Z 2020")

## SheetFlow geometry vocabulary
- Subdivided icosahedron (the hero core)
- Concentric rings (the orbit metaphor)
- Soft cubes — radius 24% of edge
- Sliced spheres — half-domes, never full
- Volumetric beams — 0.6 unit thickness, emissive 8–14%

## Material shortlist
- Matte ceramic (specular spike 6–10%)
- Brushed graphite
- Pale concrete
- Frosted glass (12% opacity, 0.8 roughness, 1.45 IOR)
- Charcoal merino (for fabric in portraits)
