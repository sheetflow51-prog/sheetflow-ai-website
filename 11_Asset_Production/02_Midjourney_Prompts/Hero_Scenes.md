# Hero Scene Prompts

For homepage / above-the-fold imagery. These are the most important prompts
in the library.

---

## hero-core-v1 — Suspended geometric core

```
wide cinematic shot of a single matte ceramic icosahedron suspended in dark
volumetric mist, lit by a large soft top-front key at 5600K with a single
hard amber rim from 90 degrees behind, deep negative space surrounding,
brushed graphite micro-satellites orbiting in the far background, 65mm
anamorphic lens, shallow depth of field, two-temperature lighting cool
sapphire and warm amber, Roger Deakins atmosphere, Apple keynote restraint,
editorial cinematography, no neon, no rgb, no holographic grids
--ar 16:9 --style raw --stylize 200 --v 6
--no neon, rgb, glow, holographic, lens flare, particles
```

## Variation strategy

Generate v1, then iterate ONE variable:
- Try the same prompt with `--ar 21:9` for ultra-cinematic
- Try with `--stylize 150` for more literal interpretation
- Try with `--seed [winning seed] --chaos 8` for related variations

Log every winner in [Prompt Library](../14_Prompt_Library/README.md).
