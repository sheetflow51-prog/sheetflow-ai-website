# Premium Sci-Fi Environment Prompts

Quiet futurism. No cyberpunk. No "tech" tropes. These are environments a
Roger Deakins would shoot in a 2030s film about an intelligence company.

## What we explicitly don't generate

- "Server room with green lights" — generic
- "Cyberpunk city street" — banned forever
- "AI face" — automatic reject
- "Hacker desk with multiple monitors" — embarrassing
- "Futuristic gym/cafe/office" — too literal

If the prompt could fit Cyberpunk 2077 marketing, it's wrong.
