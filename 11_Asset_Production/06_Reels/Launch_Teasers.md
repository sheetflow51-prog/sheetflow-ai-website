# Launch Teaser Reels

The 12–18s vertical reels used for major brand moments.

## Structure (committed)

```
0.0–1.2s   Hook frame — single cinematic still, holds without music
1.2–4.0s   Atmosphere — slow camera move on Spline scene or AI plate
4.0–7.0s   Idea — one display headline, fades in over the visual
7.0–10.0s  Reveal — the product/brand mark/proposition
10.0–14.0s Beat — quiet, single line of supporting copy
14.0–16.0s Lock — final frame holds. Sound resolves. End.
```

**Rhythm**: breath. No exceptions.

## Hook frame requirements

- Stand alone as a still
- Pure cinematic atmosphere — no copy yet
- 9:16, exported at 2160×3840 (downsamples beautifully)

## Music shape

- 0–1s silent
- 1–4s low ambient bed enters
- 4–10s build (one harmonic shift around 7s, never a drop)
- 10–14s sustain
- 14–16s resolve to silence

We do not use trap drums. We do not use risers. We do not use whoosh stacks.

## Type plan

- One headline (5 words max)
- One sub line (8 words max)
- One end-card line (4 words max — typically the brand mark)

Total reading time: ≤ 4 seconds. Type is restraint, not paragraph.

## Anti-patterns

- "Coming soon" framing
- Countdown timers
- Date stamps as graphics (use type instead)
- Multiple headlines competing
- Music that imitates Trap or hyperpop trends
