# Founder Imagery

Portraits of founders, leadership, and key contributors. The category
where SheetFlow's restraint is hardest to hold — most founder photography
is loud by default.

## Direction

Editorial, not corporate. The subject *thinks*; they don't *perform*.
Closer to a *Monocle* feature than a LinkedIn headshot.

## Composition

- **Frame:** subject occupies 35–45% of canvas
- **Eye line:** upper third
- **Negative space:** to the side the subject is looking toward
- **Background:** environmental (workspace, architecture), never seamless
- **Posture:** neutral, asymmetric. No crossed arms, no chin-on-fist.

## Lighting

- Single key from window or large soft source, 45° off subject
- Subtle warm rim from lower-back at 30% key intensity
- Fill: ambient only (or a single white card at 20%)
- Shadows preserved — never filled to gray
- Hair light forbidden

## Wardrobe

- Solid colors only: charcoal, navy, sand, off-white, deep green
- Matte fabrics only (wool, cotton, linen) — no satin, no patterns
- No logos visible
- Eyewear: matte frames only

## Skin

- Imperfections preserved. Pores visible at 100%.
- Color: neutral, slightly warm. Avoid orange skin LUTs.
- Avoid retouching that flattens texture

## What ships

- One portrait per founder for site / about page
- One environmental shot per founder for press
- One candid for social / case studies
- Optional: 1–2 group shots for team page

## What never ships

- Crossed-arms corporate
- Forced smiles toward camera
- White seamless backgrounds
- Heavy beauty retouching
- "Pointing at laptop" stock poses
- Multiple founders centered & symmetrical

## File naming

`portrait-{name-slug}-{context}-v{n}-{YYYYMMDD}.{ext}`

Examples:
- `portrait-alex-environmental-v2-20260512.png`
- `portrait-team-launch-v1-20260512.png`

## Workflow

1. Brief photographer with this README + a moodboard subset
2. Shoot raw + tethered. Color critical.
3. Cull to 8–12 per subject
4. Retouch with restraint (texture preserved)
5. Drop final selects to `_inbox/`
6. Review via `13_Asset_Review/` before any external use
