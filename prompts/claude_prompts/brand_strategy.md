# Brand Strategy Prompts

## Brand Positioning

**Purpose:** Define a sharp, differentiated brand position for SheetFlow AI
**Model:** Claude Sonnet or Opus

---

### Prompt: Brand Positioning Statement

```
You are a brand strategist specializing in B2B SaaS products. 

Help me define a clear brand positioning statement for SheetFlow AI.

Product Description:
SheetFlow AI is a spreadsheet automation platform that uses AI to help teams automate repetitive data tasks — formula writing, data cleaning, reporting, and workflow automation. It integrates with Google Sheets, Excel, and other data sources.

Target Audience:
Operations teams, finance teams, data analysts, and business users who work heavily in spreadsheets but are not developers.

Key Competitors:
- Zapier (workflow automation)
- Make.com (automation)
- Notion (workspace/database)
- Airtable (database spreadsheet hybrid)

Please provide:
1. A single clear positioning statement (1-2 sentences)
2. Three unique value propositions that differentiate us
3. The key job-to-be-done we solve better than anyone
4. Recommended brand personality attributes (5 adjectives)
5. What we are NOT (important for clarity)
```

---

### Prompt: Target Persona Development

```
Create 2-3 detailed buyer personas for SheetFlow AI, a spreadsheet automation platform.

For each persona include:
- Name and title
- Company size and industry
- Key responsibilities
- Daily pain points with spreadsheets
- Goals and success metrics
- Current tools they use
- Decision-making role (buyer, influencer, user)
- Objections they might have
- What success looks like for them

Make personas specific and realistic, not generic marketing fluff.
```

---

### Prompt: Competitive Differentiation

```
Analyze the competitive landscape for spreadsheet automation tools and help me find where SheetFlow AI should carve out a unique position.

Known competitors to analyze:
- Zapier
- Make.com
- Airtable
- Notion
- Google Apps Script
- Microsoft Power Automate

For each competitor:
1. What they do well
2. Where they fall short for spreadsheet-heavy users
3. Their positioning and messaging
4. Price point and target market

Then recommend:
- The gap in the market SheetFlow AI should own
- A positioning statement that directly contrasts with existing options
- 3 messages that would resonate vs. each major competitor
```
