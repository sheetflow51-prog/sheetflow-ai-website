## Key Design Decisions

### The Aesthetic Standard
Every design decision is evaluated against one test:
*Does this feel like Apple, Stripe, and a futuristic AI lab designed it together?*

### The Copy Standard
Every piece of copy is evaluated against one test:
*Could a competitor say this exact thing? If yes, rewrite it.*

### The Motion Standard
Every animation is evaluated against one test:
*Does this motion communicate something, or is it decoration?*

### The Technical Standard
Every component is evaluated against one test:
*Is this the most precise implementation possible, while remaining performant at 60fps?*
